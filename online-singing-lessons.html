Real Online Singing Lessons — Live 1-to-1

When you search for "online singing lessons," you get thousands of results. Most are pre-recorded video courses that can't hear your voice, can't correct your technique, and can't answer your questions. My lessons are different.

Every lesson is a live 1-to-1 video call with me, Matt Thompson. I hear you sing in real-time. I watch your technique. I give you instant feedback and personalised corrections. It's the closest thing to having me in the room with you — and you can do it from anywhere in the world.

Lessons are available via FaceTime, WhatsApp, or Microsoft Teams — whichever platform works best for you. No special equipment needed. Just a device with a camera and a quiet space.

How Online Singing Lessons Work

It's simple. No complicated software. No confusing apps:

  1. Book your lesson: Choose a consultation, single lesson, or block. Pick a time that works for you.
  2. Receive your link: You'll get a meeting link automatically via email. No account needed.
  3. Join the live call: Click the link at your scheduled time. I see you, you see me. We start singing.
  4. Get your recording: After the lesson, I send you a recording and personalised practice notes.

All lessons are recorded and sent to you for practice between sessions.
