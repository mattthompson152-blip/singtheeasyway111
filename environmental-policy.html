Environmental Policy

Our commitment to sustainability.

Last updated: 22 March 2026

1. Our Commitment

Sing The Easy Way is committed to minimising our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of our business operations.

2. Digital-First Approach

By teaching exclusively online, we eliminate:

  • Student and teacher commuting emissions
  • Physical studio energy consumption
  • Paper-based lesson materials (all digital)
  • Physical merchandise and packaging waste

3. Mobile Studio Operations

Our teaching studio operates from a motorhome with the following environmental considerations:

  • Solar power integration where possible
  • Energy-efficient equipment and lighting
  • Minimal water usage and waste production
  • Responsible disposal of all waste materials

4. Carbon Footprint Reduction

We actively work to reduce our carbon footprint by:

  • Using renewable energy providers where available
  • Minimising unnecessary travel
  • Choosing energy-efficient technology
  • Offsetting unavoidable emissions through verified carbon offset schemes

5. Sustainable Partnerships

We prioritise working with service providers who demonstrate environmental responsibility, including:

  • Green web hosting providers
  • Payment processors with carbon reduction commitments
  • Digital service providers over physical alternatives

6. Continuous Improvement

We regularly review our environmental practices and seek opportunities to:

  • Reduce energy consumption
  • Minimise waste generation
  • Improve resource efficiency
  • Support environmental causes

7. Compliance

We comply with all applicable environmental legislation and regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate.

8. Review

This environmental policy is reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect changes in our operations and environmental best practices.

9. Contact

For questions about our environmental policy, please contact:
