What Makes Live 1-to-1 Singing Lessons Different

There's a world of difference between watching a video and singing with a teacher who can hear you. Live 1-to-1 singing lessons give you something no pre-recorded course ever can: real-time, personalised feedback on your actual voice.

During every lesson, I listen to you sing live. I watch your posture, your breathing, your mouth shape. When something isn't quite right, I stop you immediately and show you exactly how to fix it. When you nail it, I tell you straight away so you know what "right" feels like. This instant feedback loop is why my students improve so quickly.

These are not pre-recorded videos. Every session is a live video call where you and I work together in real-time. You can ask questions whenever they come up. You can try a technique, get feedback, adjust, and try again — all within the same minute. That's the power of live 1-to-1.

How Live 1-to-1 Online Lessons Work

It's simpler than you think. No fancy equipment. No complicated apps:

  1. Book your live session: Choose a time that works for you. I'll send you a confirmation with a meeting link.
  2. Join the live call: At your scheduled time, click the link. I see you, you see me. We start singing together.
  3. Get live feedback: I listen to you sing, watch your technique, and give you instant corrections and encouragement.
  4. Receive your recording: After the lesson, I send you a recording and personalised practice notes so you know exactly what to work on.

Available via FaceTime, WhatsApp, or Microsoft Teams. No account required for Teams — just click the link in your browser.
