About Matt

25+ years of professional vocal coaching experience.

Meet Your Vocal Coach

Matt Thompson

Matt Thompson has spent over two decades helping singers find their voice. From complete beginners to recording artists, film stars, TV personalities, sports personalities, actors, and Bollywood performers—Matt's approach is tailored to each individual's goals and abilities.

Now teaching exclusively online from a mobile studio, Matt brings professional vocal coaching directly to you—wherever you are in the world.

Teaching Philosophy

Singing should feel natural and enjoyable. Matt's "Easy Way" method breaks down complex techniques into simple, actionable steps that build confidence and produce real results.
