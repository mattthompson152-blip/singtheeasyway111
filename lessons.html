Online Singing Lessons

One-to-one virtual vocal coaching tailored to your goals.

How I Teach

Personalised Online Vocal Coaching

Every voice is unique. My approach adapts to your individual needs, whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned performer looking to refine your technique.

Lessons take place online via FaceTime, WhatsApp, or Microsoft Teams — whichever works best for you. All you need is a quiet space and a device with a camera.

When you book a lesson, you'll receive a Microsoft Teams appointment automatically. Simply click the link in your confirmation email to join the lesson at your scheduled time. No Teams account required - you can join directly through your web browser. You'll also receive reminder notifications before your lesson starts.

What You'll Learn

🎵 Vocal Technique

Breath control, posture, pitch accuracy, and vocal health. Build a solid foundation for your voice.

🎭 Performance Skills

Stage presence, emotional connection, and storytelling through song. Bring your performances to life.

🎤 Repertoire Building

Find songs that suit your voice and develop a diverse setlist for auditions or performances.

🎧 Recording Technique

Learn to sing in the studio with microphone technique and vocal production tips.

Lesson Structure

Each session follows a proven format to maximise your progress:

  1. Warm-up (10 mins): Vocal exercises to prepare your voice and prevent strain.
  2. Technique Work (20 mins): Focused exercises targeting specific areas for improvement.
  3. Repertoire (25 mins): Applying techniques to songs of your choice.
  4. Cool-down & Notes (5 mins): Summary of key points and practice recommendations.

All lessons are recorded and sent to you for practice between sessions.
